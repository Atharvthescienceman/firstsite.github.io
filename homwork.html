MY FAVORITES:



Six Flags


Six Flags Entertainment Corporation is a regional theme park operator. The Company owns and operates approximately 27 regional theme and water parks. Its parks occupy approximately 6,000 acres of land and is located in geographically diverse markets across North America. six flags is also home to the lattest and scariest ride in the world.

Lake Geaorge

The Battle of Lake George was fought on September 8, 1755. British forces of 1,500 soldiers and 200 Mohawk Indians under the command of William Johnson defeated a much larger allied French and Indian force of 3,500 under German General Baron Dieskau. lake george is also one of the finest places to go cruising.

Liberty Science Center

The Science Center houses 12 museum exhibition halls, a live animal collection with 110 species, giant aquariums, a 3D theater, live simulcast surgeries, hurricane- and tornado-force wind simulators, K-12 classrooms and labs. it is the best and funnest place to explore the world of science.
